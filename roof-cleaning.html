Protect Your Investment

Why Roof Cleaning Matters

A dirty roof is more than an eyesore. Left untreated, algae and organic growth can cause real damage to your home.

Algae Eats Shingles

Gloeocapsa Magma feeds on the limestone filler in asphalt shingles, breaking them down over time.

Shortens Roof Life

Unchecked algae and moss growth can cut years off your roof's expected lifespan, leading to premature replacement.

Hurts Curb Appeal

Black streaks and green patches make even a well-maintained home look neglected and run down.

Affects Home Value

A stained roof signals deferred maintenance to buyers and appraisers, potentially reducing your home's market value.
