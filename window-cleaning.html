Why It Matters

Why Professional Window Cleaning Makes a Difference

Florida's climate is tough on windows. Salt air deposits a hazy film. Hard water from sprinklers leaves mineral spots that etch into the glass over time. Pollen, dust, and humidity coat every surface year-round. The longer these sit, the harder they are to remove and the more damage they do.

Removes hard water stains before they permanently etch glass
Clears salt film, pollen, and dust buildup
Extends the life of your window seals and frames
Lets more natural light into your home
Improves curb appeal for guests and potential buyers
Catches early signs of seal failure, rot, or damage

DIY vs. Professional Cleaning

✕ Garden hose leaves streaks and water spots
✕ Store-bought cleaners can damage tinting and coatings
✕ Second-story windows are dangerous without proper equipment
✕ Screens and tracks get skipped
