Our Story

Built on Hard Work and Honest Service

Bright Sight Pro Services was started by people who live here and understand what South Florida weather does to homes. The heat, the rain, the salt air, the humidity. It all adds up fast. We saw too many homeowners paying for work that did not last or dealing with companies that were hard to reach. So we decided to do it differently.

We are a local company based right here in Palm Beach Gardens. Every home we clean is in our own community, and we treat it that way. We show up on time, use the right equipment for the job, and make sure the results speak for themselves. No cutting corners, no surprise charges, no disappearing after the first visit.

Whether we are washing windows, cleaning a roof, or restoring a driveway, the goal is always the same: leave your property looking better than we found it and make the whole experience easy for you. That is the standard we hold ourselves to on every single job.

Local Expertise

Why Palm Beach County

South Florida is not like anywhere else. The salt air corrodes surfaces. The humidity feeds algae and mold growth on roofs, walls, and walkways. Tropical sun fades and bakes everything it touches. And then there are the storms, the pollen, and the hard water that leaves mineral deposits on every window.

We know exactly what Palm Beach County homes are up against because we live here too. We understand the specific cleaning methods, products, and schedules that work for this climate. A roof in Palm Beach Gardens needs different care than one up north. A driveway in Jupiter takes a beating that homes in other states never see.

Many of our customers also have HOA requirements to meet. We help you stay compliant and keep your home looking sharp without the stress. Whether it is a yearly roof cleaning, quarterly window service, or a full exterior refresh before season, we know what your home needs and when it needs it.
