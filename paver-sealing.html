What's Included

Every paver sealing job is handled with care, from start to finish. Here is what you get with every project.

Driveway, Patio & Pool Deck Pavers

We seal all paver surfaces around your home, including driveways, patios, backyards, and pool decks.

Thorough Pre-Clean

Every surface is pressure washed and cleaned before sealing, removing dirt, algae, and debris so the sealer bonds properly.

Sand Joint Restoration

We re-sand paver joints with polymeric sand to stabilize the surface, prevent shifting, and lock everything in place.

Color-Enhancing & Matte Options

Stain & Moisture Block

Professional-grade sealers create a protective barrier against oil stains, rust, and moisture penetration.

Weed, Ant & UV Protection

Sealed joints prevent weed growth and ant intrusion, while UV-resistant sealers protect your pavers from sun damage and fading.

Why It Matters

Why Seal Your Pavers?

South Florida is beautiful, but it is tough on your outdoor surfaces. Without proper protection, pavers break down faster than most homeowners expect.

Florida's intense UV rays fade paver color quickly, leaving surfaces looking washed out and dull
Heavy rain erodes joint sand and causes pavers to shift, crack, and become uneven over time
Warm, humid conditions promote mold, mildew, and algae growth between and on top of pavers
Unsealed pavers deteriorate significantly faster, leading to costly replacements instead of simple maintenance
