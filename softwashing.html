Softwashing Services in Palm Beach Gardens

Unlike pressure washing, softwashing uses low-pressure water combined with specialized biodegradable cleaning solutions to safely remove algae, mold, mildew, and grime. It is the preferred method for delicate surfaces like vinyl siding, stucco, painted exteriors, and screen enclosures because it cleans without the risk of damage.

Vinyl & Aluminum Siding

The Smarter Clean

Why Softwashing Instead of Pressure Washing?

Pressure washing blasts surfaces with high-pressure water. While effective on concrete and pavers, it can cause serious damage to siding, stucco, and screen enclosures. Softwashing takes a different approach by combining low-pressure water with professional-grade cleaning solutions that do the heavy lifting.

Won't crack or chip stucco surfaces
Won't blow out or tear screen enclosure mesh
Won't strip paint from wood or trim
Won't force water behind siding panels
Kills algae, mold, and mildew spores at the root
Results last 3 to 4 times longer than pressure washing

Pressure Washing on Delicate Surfaces

  • ✕ High pressure can crack stucco and chip paint
  • ✕ Tears and stretches screen enclosure mesh
  • ✕ Only removes surface growth, regrows quickly

Softwashing (Our Method)

  • ✓ Safe for siding, stucco, screens, and paint

  • ✓ Results last significantly longer
  • ✓ Eco-friendly, biodegradable solutions
