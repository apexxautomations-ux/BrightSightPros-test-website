## Logo

**Primary Logo:** Full color horizontal lockup featuring the window/squeegee icon with "Bright Sight" wordmark and "PRO SERVICES" subtitle.

### Logo Usage Rules

- Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "P" in PRO SERVICES on all sides
- Never stretch, distort, rotate, or recolor the logo
- Never place the full color logo on a busy background without a solid or semi-transparent backing
- Minimum display size: 1 inch wide for print, 120px wide for digital

### Logo Variations

| Variation | Use Case |
|-----------|----------|
| Full color on white | Default for all materials |
| Full color on dark background | Use white backing or reversed version if available |
| Single color (primary blue) | When color printing is not available |

## Voice and Tone

### Brand Personality

Bright Sight Pro Services is **professional, straightforward, and trustworthy**. We talk like a knowledgeable neighbor, not a corporate brand. We are confident without being pushy.

### Voice Principles

1. **Clear over clever** — Say what we do and why it matters. No jargon, no fluff.
2. **Confident** — We know our craft. Speak with authority, not arrogance.
3. **Outcome focused** — Always lead with the result the homeowner gets, not the process or equipment.
4. **Friendly but professional** — Warm enough to feel approachable, professional enough to feel reliable.

### Writing Rules

- Lead with the homeowner's problem or desired outcome
- Use short sentences and short paragraphs
- Do not use all caps for emphasis (use bold sparingly)
- Avoid technical jargon unless explaining a process to educate
- Do not use exclamation marks more than once per piece of content
- Use "your home" and "you" language, not "our services" or "we offer"

### Example Phrasing

| Instead of this | Write this |
|-----------------|-----------|
| We offer premium pressure washing services | Your driveway looking rough? We will have it looking brand new |
| Our team utilizes advanced soft washing techniques | Soft washing removes algae and mold without damaging your surfaces |
| Contact us today for a free quote! | Get your free quote in under 2 minutes |

## Photography and Imagery

### Style

- **Before/after shots** are the highest priority content. Always show dramatic transformations.
- Use natural daylight. No heavy filters or oversaturated edits.
- Show real jobs, real results. Avoid stock photography whenever possible.
- Frame shots to show the full scope of the work (windows, driveways, roofs, gutters, walkways).

### What to Capture

- Before and after (side by side or slider format)
- Action shots of work being done (pressure washing spray, team on site)
- Close ups of clean surfaces showing texture and detail
- The truck, trailer, or equipment (branded if possible)

### What to Avoid

- Blurry or dark photos
- Cluttered backgrounds that distract from the clean result
- Photos with visible competitor branding
- Stock photos of models posing as cleaners

## Advertising Guidelines

### Ad Creative Rules

- Never lead with price in ad copy
- Lead with the problem or transformation, not the service name
- Use before/after imagery as the primary creative format
- Every ad should have one clear CTA
- Landing pages should use a lead form before any calendar booking

### Ad Copy Framework

**Hook** — Call out the problem or paint the picture of the result
**Body** — Brief explanation of how we solve it
**CTA** — One clear next step (get a quote, see pricing, book now)

### Example Ad Copy

> Your driveway has seen better days. Mold, grime, and tire marks making your home look older than it is? We bring it back to life in one visit. Tap below to get your free quote.
